About Me

I have an MSc in Physics, with a thesis on the Monte Carlo simulation of a particle detector of the ATLAS experiment at CERN, which provided me with strong logical, mathematical and computer skills.

In 2016, I started a collaboration with the Robotics Research Center “E.Piaggio” of the University of Pisa, where I started a Ph.D. in 2017. I am currently part of Roboteam-Italia, one of the teams of Roborace, an autonomous full scale electric racing car competition.

I have always had a strong inclination toward software development (and in particular to object oriented design), which led me to delve into the development of the team driving simulator, based on Unreal Engine 4, which the team uses to test the whole perception, planning and control stack.
